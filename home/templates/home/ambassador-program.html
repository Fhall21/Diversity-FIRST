Be part of the movement



We are excited to have you part of this journey!

Part of celebrating diversity is getting everyone on board. We know you want to work on your robot and sometimes doing impactful outreach can be hard... so we've made a program which makes the most of your time.


Why join? Well it can give strong outreach benefits by:

Giving something you can talk about for outreach

Giving you a voice with promoting diversity within FIRST

Getting you a cool logo on that T-Shirt of yours :)

Giving you outreach bragging rights because you're actually doing something cool


Is it much work? Nope, all you would have to:

  1. Print and laminate an A3 #diversityFIRST poster
  2. Find teams during your match day or similar
  3. Get them to write how they do diversity on a #diversityFIRST poster
  4. Take a photo with the team and repeat
  5. Upload photos here or share on instagram with the tag: #diversityFIRST
